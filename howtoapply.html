Quick Micro-loans For Students

The fastest and easiest way to get loans on campus. How? Just follow the steps below

Contact Us

  1. Call or WhatsApp our contact numbers. We can also be reached via our Instagram Account.
  2. Let us know how much and the duration you want the money for (Maximum period is One month).
  3. Send pictures of the item you want to use as collateral for the loan.

Processing

  1. Our Assessment Department appraises the item and we get back to you with an offer.
  2. Accept or decline the offer.

Note: Your request takes a maximum of 2hrs to be processed.

Loan Disbursement

  1. An appointment is scheduled for you to come over with the collateral for the money.

It is VERY important that you are punctual to the appointment. Do not be late!
